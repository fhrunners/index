Run With Us

Looking forward to seeing you!

Running with us is always free! Come to one of our runs and find out what FHR is all about!

Monday Social / Recovery Run

6:30PM at the Forest Hills Bike Rotary

Join us for a relaxed social / recovery run every Monday evening through the SW Corridor and around Jamaica Pond!

Route: Starting at the Forest Hills Bike Rotary, we'll run north along the SW Corridor bike path, then turn onto Boylston street, and end with a lap around most of Jamaica Pond!

Average Pace: ~10:00 min/mile.

Meetup Location: Forest Hills Bike Rotary, in front of the Forest Hills T station's North Entrance. View on Google Maps

Parking/Getting Here: You can reach the meetup spot via public transit by taking the Orange Line / Commuter Rail / bus to the Forest Hills T stop. [Ask FHR folks about parking!]

Thursday Track Night

7PM at the Downes Field Track (Winter: 7:30PM at the Reggie Lewis Center)

Improve your speed and technique with our Thursday track nights! All levels are welcome and no track experience is needed. We'll start with a brief warmup jog, followed by some dynamic warm-ups and stretches, then break out into workouts.

If you don't have a workout in mind, you can join up with someone else, or we can suggest one for you! (e.g., 400m repeats, jogging around the track/Jamaica Pond, hill repeats along Jamaicaway).

Average Pace: Up to you! You can bring your own workout, join someone else's, or do some laps around the track.

Meetup Location: Downes Field Track, near the picnic tables on the southwest side of the track. (Winter: Reggie Lewis Center)

The Saturday Run

8:30AM at the Forest Hills Bike Rotary

Join us for a run every Saturday morning, followed by coffee (and ice cream!) at J.P. Licks! We'll start off with a round of names, then split up into groups based on how far and fast people want to run.

Route: Various routes around the Forest Hills area; groups usually pick routes based on how far they want to go.

Average Pace: We typically have groups ranging from ~10:30+ min/mile for ~4 miles up to ~8:30 min/mile for 8+ miles, but it depends on what people want to do that day. (If you want to go a shorter distance, folks will often peel off and head to J.P. Licks earlier!)

Meetup Location: Forest Hills Bike Rotary, in front of the Forest Hills T station's North Entrance. View on Google Maps

Parking/Getting Here: You can reach the meetup spot via public transit by taking the Orange Line / Commuter Rail / bus to the Forest Hills T stop. [Ask FHR folks about parking!]
