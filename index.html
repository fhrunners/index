Forest Hills Runners

Community running for all, completely free

Come run with us!

Founded in April of 2011, Forest Hills Runners is a running group based in Jamaica Plain, MA. It's community running at its best, bringing people together from all across JP. It's for Olympians and life-long runners, and it's for people who have never run a step in their lives but would love to meet others and give running a try.

Our first run together was on Tuesday April 26th, 2011. We continue to run strong.
