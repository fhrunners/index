Who We Are

Forest Hills Runners is more than just a running club; it’s a community. Founded with the mission to provide free, accessible running opportunities, FHR welcomes everyone, regardless of their experience or pace. Whether you're a beginner looking to start your running journey or an experienced marathoner, you’ll find a place here.

Our Runs

We offer a variety of runs throughout the week to fit different schedules and running preferences:

  • Monday Social / Recovery Run: A relaxed social run every Monday evening through the SW Corridor and around Jamaica Pond. 6:30PM at the Forest Hills Bike Rotary.
  • Thursday Track Night: Improve your speed and technique with interval-based workouts. 7PM at the Downes Field Track (Winter: 7:30PM at the Reggie Lewis Center).
  • The Saturday Run: Various pace groups for a long run, followed by coffee at J.P. Licks. 8:30AM at the Forest Hills Bike Rotary.

Community Engagement

Forest Hills Runners is deeply committed to the local community. We partner with organizations such as the Franklin Park Coalition and the Hyde Square Task Force, enhancing our community engagement and support.

Join Us

Running with FHR is always free, and we welcome new members at any time. Check out our Run With Us page for details on our runs. For the latest updates on our runs and events, follow us on Instagram and join our Facebook group.

By fostering a sense of belonging and inclusivity, FHR promotes physical fitness and strengthens the Jamaica Plain community. We look forward to running with you soon!
